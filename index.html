Safe Inbox

Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Matplotlib

I processed and cleaned 800K+ records, engineered URL-based features, and trained multiple models including Random Forest, XGBoost, and Logistic Regression. After tuning and comparison, the final model achieved ~95% classification accuracy.
